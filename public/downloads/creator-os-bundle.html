Section 01

How to Use This Bundle

This bundle is not a collection of tips — it is a complete operating architecture for your creator business. Every system connects to every other system. Follow the setup order to get maximum value.

⚡
Implementation order matters. Build Systems 1 → 5 in sequence. Each system feeds the next. Skipping ahead creates gaps that cost time later.

The Creator OS Philosophy

Most creators operate reactively — responding to trends, creating content on the fly, managing their business from their inbox. The Creator OS flips this. You operate proactively, from systems, with your time protected at every layer.

The Four Principles
1. Capture everything. No idea, task, or insight lives in your head. It goes into the system.

2. Process weekly. One 90-minute weekly review keeps everything current. Nothing falls through.

3. Automate the repeatable. If you do it more than once, it should happen automatically.

4. Review and adjust. Monthly review: what worked, what didn't, what changes.

Section 02

The 5 Creator Systems

Your creator business runs on five interdependent systems. Miss one and you feel it everywhere.

📡
System 1 — Content Engine
Ideation → Creation → Publishing → Repurposing
Core
  • Content Idea Bank (Notion database capturing all ideas with status, format, platform)
  • Content Brief template (hook, outline, CTA, thumbnail concept, repurpose plan)
  • Editorial calendar with rolling 4-week view
  • Publishing checklist per platform (YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • Repurposing matrix: every long-form piece → 8+ short-form assets automatically
  • Performance tracking: views, engagement rate, revenue attribution per piece
💰
System 2 — Revenue Engine
Income streams → Tracking → Optimization
Revenue
  • Income stream tracker (products, brand deals, AdSense, subscriptions, services)
  • Monthly P&L dashboard with automated formulas
  • Brand deal pipeline (prospect → outreach → negotiation → signed → delivered → paid)
  • Product launch calendar integrated with content calendar
  • Revenue target tracker with progress bars and milestone alerts
  • Tax preparation bucket (auto-flag income over thresholds)
⚙️
System 3 — Operations Hub
Tasks → Projects → People → Processes
Ops
  • Master task inbox (capture → triage → assign → track)
  • Project database with status, deadline, priority, and linked tasks
  • SOP library — every repeatable process documented once, used forever
  • Vendor/contractor database (name, rate, specialty, portfolio, contact)
  • Weekly review template (accomplishments, blockers, next week priorities)
  • Daily shutdown checklist ensuring clean handoff to tomorrow-you
📚
System 4 — Learning Vault
Input → Processing → Application → Retention
Growth
  • Book notes database (title, key insights, action items, date read)
  • Course & video tracker with implementation status
  • Idea → Content pipeline (insights that become content pieces)
  • Second Brain inbox for web clips, articles, and research
  • Monthly learning review: what did I apply this month?
  • Skills development tracker with target levels and resources
🎯
System 5 — Goals & Vision
Vision → Annual → Quarterly → Weekly
Strategy
  • 3-year vision document (revenue, audience, lifestyle, impact targets)
  • Annual goal breakdown with quarterly milestones
  • OKR framework: objectives with 3 key results each, graded quarterly
  • Not-to-do list (as important as your to-do list)
  • Monthly scorecard: rate each area of business 1–10, identify gaps
  • Annual review template (wins, lessons, eliminations, upgrades)

Essential Notion Formulas

Content Scoring Formula (Ideas Database)

// Scores an idea 1-100 based on effort vs. potential if(prop("Potential") > 7 and prop("Effort") < 4, "🔥 High Priority", if(prop("Potential") > 5, "📊 Medium Priority", "🗄️ Archive" ) )

Weekly Revenue Rollup (Revenue Database)

// Sum revenue entries where date is within current week formatDate(now(), "W") == formatDate(prop("Date"), "W")

Project Health Score (Projects Database)

// Returns Red/Yellow/Green based on deadline proximity if(dateBetween(prop("Deadline"), now(), "days") < 3, "🔴 At Risk", if(dateBetween(prop("Deadline"), now(), "days") < 7, "🟡 Watch", "🟢 On Track" ) )

Content Performance Rating (Content Database)

// Grades content A–D based on views vs. your average if(prop("Views") > prop("Channel Avg") * 2, "A", if(prop("Views") > prop("Channel Avg"), "B", if(prop("Views") > prop("Channel Avg") * 0.5, "C", "D") ) )

Recommended Views to Build

📅 Today View
Filter: Tasks where Due Date = Today AND Status ≠ Done. Sort by Priority. This is your daily cockpit.
📡 Content Pipeline
Kanban view of Content database: Idea → Script → Recorded → Edited → Scheduled → Published. Drag between columns.
💰 Revenue Dashboard
Gallery view grouped by Month. Formula property calculates running monthly total. Link to Google Sheets for charts.
🔥 Weekly Priority Board
Filter: Tasks created this week OR due this week. Group by Project. Shows exactly where your week's effort goes.
Section 04

10 Essential Automations

These automations collectively save 10–15 hours per week once set up. Each one runs forever with zero maintenance.

1
New YouTube Video → Auto-create repurposing tasks
Tool: Make · Trigger: YouTube channel — new video published · Action: Create 8 tasks in Notion (Instagram Reel, LinkedIn post, Twitter thread, Short, Blog post, Newsletter section, Quote card, Pinterest pin) all linked to the original video, due in 48 hours.
2
Brand deal inquiry email → CRM entry
Tool: Zapier · Trigger: Gmail — new email matching "brand deal" OR "partnership" OR "collaboration" · Action: Create record in Notion Contacts database with brand name, email, and status "Inbound Lead." Tag for weekly review.
3
Stripe payment → Revenue log + celebration
Tool: Make · Trigger: Stripe — payment succeeded · Action: Log to Notion Revenue database AND Google Sheets tab. If amount > $500, send Slack/Discord notification "💰 $X sale just landed."
4
Weekly performance report auto-generated
Tool: Make · Trigger: Schedule — every Monday 8am · Action: Pull YouTube Analytics, Stripe revenue, and Notion task completion %. Compile into a formatted email/Slack message. No manual pulling numbers.
5
Content idea saved to Notion from anywhere
Tool: Zapier · Trigger: Email to ideas@yourdomain.com OR specific hashtag in notes app · Action: Create record in Notion Ideas database with captured text, date, and status "New." Review in weekly session.
6
Overdue tasks → Morning digest
Tool: Make · Trigger: Schedule — every weekday 7am · Action: Query Notion for tasks where Due Date is past AND Status ≠ Done. Format as a clean list and send to email/SMS. Urgency filter: tasks 3+ days overdue flagged in red.
7
New subscriber → Welcome sequence trigger
Tool: Zapier · Trigger: ConvertKit/Beehiiv — new subscriber · Action: Tag subscriber based on which lead magnet they used. Enroll in corresponding 5-email welcome sequence. Add to segment in CRM.
8
Monthly revenue report to Google Sheets
Tool: Make · Trigger: Schedule — 1st of each month · Action: Pull all Notion Revenue entries from prior month. Calculate totals by category. Append row to Google Sheets dashboard. Update running annual total chart automatically.
9
Published content → Social cross-post notifications
Tool: Make · Trigger: Notion Content database — status changed to "Published" · Action: Send reminder notifications to post to secondary platforms within 2 hours. Include caption template prefilled from the content brief.
10
Weekly content schedule → Calendar blocks
Tool: Make · Trigger: Schedule — every Friday 5pm · Action: Pull next week's content from Notion. Create Google Calendar events for each creation/recording/editing session with 2-hour blocks. No manual scheduling needed.

Weekly Review Template (90 minutes every Sunday)

📋 Weekly Review
CAPTURE (20 min)
→ Clear all inboxes: email, notes, voice memos, texts, paper
→ Every open loop goes into Notion

CLARIFY (20 min)
→ Process every new item: is it actionable? By when? By whom?
→ Assign due dates, priorities, and projects

REVIEW (30 min)
→ Check all active projects: what's the next action?
→ Review last week: what did I complete? What slipped? Why?
→ Revenue check: where am I vs. monthly goal?

PLAN (20 min)
→ Identify the 3 most important tasks for next week
→ Block time on the calendar for deep work
→ Set content publishing schedule for the week

90-Day Implementation Roadmap

Follow this sequence exactly. Each phase builds on the last.

Week 1–2
Foundation: Build the Notion Workspace
  • Create 6 master databases (Tasks, Projects, Content, Ideas, Revenue, Contacts)
  • Set up 4 essential views per database
  • Enter all current active projects and tasks
  • Migrate existing content archive
    • Week 3–4
    Revenue: Wire the Money System
  • Set up Revenue database and Google Sheets mirror
  • Log all existing income streams
  • Connect Stripe → Notion automation (Automation #3)
  • Establish monthly revenue target and tracking
    • Week 5–6
    Content: Activate the Content Engine
  • Build content calendar with 4-week rolling view
  • Set up YouTube → repurposing tasks automation (Automation #1)
  • Create first 10 content briefs using the template
  • Establish publishing rhythm (days/times per platform)
    • Week 7–8
    Automations: Deploy All 10
  • Build automations 1–5 in Make
  • Build automations 6–10 in Zapier or Make
  • Test each automation with live data
  • Document any custom steps in your SOP library
    • Week 9–10
    Refinement: Weekly Review Habit
  • Complete first 2 full weekly reviews using the template
  • Identify what's working, what needs adjustment
  • Prune any unused databases or views
  • Document your personal SOPs based on your workflow
    • Week 11–12
    Optimization: Review and Accelerate
  • 90-day retrospective: what revenue did the system generate?
  • Identify highest-leverage content from performance data
  • Set Q2 goals using the OKR framework
  • Hire/delegate based on what the ops system surfaces
    • 🏆
    By Day 90: You have a fully operational creator business that runs from systems, not willpower. Your content compounds, your revenue is visible, and your time is protected. This is the unfair advantage.
