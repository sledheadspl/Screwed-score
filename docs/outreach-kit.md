## 2. Walk-in one-pager (print, hand to owner/manager)

**Headline:** Your restaurant is probably overpaying $3,000–$8,000/yr on vendor bills you've stopped reading.

**Body:**
- Card processing: hidden tier bumps, PCI fees, "non-qualified" surcharges
- Linen / uniform: auto-renewals at 8–12% above market
- Waste: container size never right-sized after volume changed
- POS: paying for features you don't use
- Pest, grease, software subscriptions: the silent bleed

**What we do:** Send us 3 months of bills. In 48 hours you get a line-itemed report: what's a fair rate, what to renegotiate, scripts for the calls. $497 flat. If we don't find $1,500+/yr in savings, full refund.

**Why us:** Built screwedscore.com — the AI that's audited [N] consumer bills. Same tech, B2B version.

**Next step:** screwedscore.com/business-audit or text [phone].

## 3. TikTok hook (4.5K followers — leverage the audience)

**Hook (0–3s):** "Your restaurant is losing $5,000 a year and you don't even know."

**Beat 2 (3–10s):** Show a real merchant statement (blur the name). Point to "non-qualified surcharge," "PCI compliance fee," "monthly minimum."

**Beat 3 (10–25s):** "These three lines on your processor statement? Negotiable. Every one. Most owners never look because the bill auto-pays."

**Beat 4 (25–40s):** "I built a tool that reads your bills and tells you exactly what's BS and what to say to get it removed. $497 flat. Refund if I don't find at least $1,500."

**CTA:** "Link in bio. Restaurants only this month."

**Variants to test:**
- Linen contract auto-renewal trap
- Waste hauler overage scam
- POS lock-in fees
